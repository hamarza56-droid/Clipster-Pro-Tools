the pipeline

Five stages. One screen.

Every clip moves through the same checkpoints — you can see exactly where each one is, for every campaign, at a glance.

01

Upload

Drop in your clip, one at a time or in bulk for a whole batch.

02

Verify logo

Checks the clip against the campaign's reference logo before you waste time on one that won't qualify.

03

Swap background

Shrinks the clip onto a 9:16 canvas and fills the border with a color, blur, or your own image.

04

Post

Download the export and post it yourself — your account stays in your control.

05

Submit

Paste the live link back in and track submission status against the campaign.
